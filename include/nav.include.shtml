JuxtaPros is not about the decisions, not the data. Once you've made your decision, you shouldn't be second-guessing yourself. The purpose of brainstorming is not to collect data, but to mull over ideas.

If you're looking for a persistent storage location for pros and cons, this isn't it. (At least not yet.) If needed, though, you could print the page to a PDF. It doesn't look terrible.
